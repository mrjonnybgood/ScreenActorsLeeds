=== WHERE TO PUT IMAGES ===

Upload your images into this specific folder: 
public/images/

=== REQUIRED FILENAMES ===
(The code looks for these exact names)

1. logo.png               (Your main logo)
2. phil-photo.jpg         (Headshot for About page)
3. kirkstall-exterior.jpg (Building outside shot)
4. kirkstall-interior.jpg (Building inside shot)
5. workshop-scene.jpg     (Action shot: Scene work)
6. workshop-camera.jpg    (Action shot: Camera work)
7. workshop-feedback.jpg  (Action shot: Feedback/Group)

=== NOTE ===
If you do not upload a file, the website will automatically use a high-quality placeholder image from the internet so the site doesn't look broken.
